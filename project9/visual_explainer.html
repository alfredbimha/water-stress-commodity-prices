Understanding Water Stress & Commodity Analysis

Does water scarcity predict agricultural commodity prices?

The Water-Food Nexus

Agriculture consumes ~70% of global freshwater. When water becomes scarce, crop yields fall, and commodity prices rise. But can we measure this relationship quantitatively?

Granger Causality

Does past drought data help predict future wheat prices? If adding drought lags improves the forecast, we say drought "Granger-causes" wheat prices.

VAR Analysis

We model water stress and commodity returns as a system — each variable can influence the others with a lag of days to months.
